Tapjack!

(Like Slapjack but with your keyboard!)

Game and Rules:

  • The goal of Tapjack is to have all the cards in your hand.
  • Each player will place a card down (using "Q" or "[") until they see a "J - Jack" card.
  • When a Jack is placed, both players will try to tap their "Jack!" key ("W" or "]") as fast as they can!
  • Whoever taps their key the fastest wins all the cards that were placed (aka, the pile)!
  • The process repeats until one player has all the cards.
  • If a player taps their Jack! key before a Jack is on screen, that player will be penalized for 2 turns and cannot use their Jack! key.
